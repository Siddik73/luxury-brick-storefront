# Design System: Brutalist Luxury

## Visual Identity
The aesthetic blends high-fashion editorial design with raw industrial brutalism. Ultra-high contrast, minimalist, and premium.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `Void Black` (#0A0A0A) - Deep, absolute black for maximum contrast.
- **Primary**: `Ember Red` (#D83528) - Used only for the product (the brick) and critical Call-to-Actions.
- **Text Primary**: `Bone White` (#F0F0F0) - Crisp, high-contrast typography.
- **Text Secondary**: `Ash Gray` (#8A8A8A) - For supporting information and meta-text.
- **Accent**: `Muted Gold` (#C5A059) - Used sparingly for price tags and decorative underlines.
- **Surface**: `Onyx Black` (#161616) - For cards, containers, and elevated UI sections.

## Design Rules
- **No Gradients**: All colors must be flat and solid.
- **No Drop Shadows**: Avoid text shadows or soft glows; maintain sharp edges.
- **Contrast**: Enforce ultra-high contrast ratios between backgrounds and foreground elements.
- **Typography**: 
    - Headings: Serif (Playfair Display or Bodoni style) for a luxury feel.
    - Body: Clean Sans-Serif (Inter or Roboto) for technical clarity.
- **Grid & Spacing**: Generous whitespace, brutalist alignment, and sharp borders.
